Molly Makes Drinks

Cocktails for Everyone

(and Every Body )

Molly Flynn loves drinks, especially craft cocktails. When Molly went sober in 2016, she had a hard time finding any suitable replacements for her favorite pastime. Fast forward to the pandemic and all of a sudden one zero proof spirit emerged... then another ...then another! Molly found a new passion for transforming all her favorite beverages into non-alcoholic cocktails utilizing alcohol-free spirits. Her favorite cocktail these days is the same as before:
an Old Fashioned.

Follow along with Molly as she makes alcohol-free
drinks that are delicious AF.
